Publications

[NeurIPS 2019 (Spotlight)] Policy Continuation with Hindsight Inverse Dynamics [Preprint][Code] [Homepage]
Hao Sun, Zhizhong Li, Xiaotong Liu, Dahua Lin, Bolei Zhou

  • We proposed a new approach called Policy Continuation with Hindsight Inverse Dynamics (PCHID) for reward sparse goal-oriented tasks. This approach learns from Hindsight Inverse Dynamics based on Hindsight Experience Replay and can work in isolation or be combined with other on-policy and off-policy algorithms. On two multi-goal tasks, PCHID significantly improves the sample efficiency as well as the final performance.

    • [NeurIPS 2019 DeepRL.W] Learning with Uniqueness through Social Constraint
    Hao Sun, Jiankai Sun, Zhenghao Peng, Dahua Lin, Bolei Zhou

  • In this work, we introduce social influence into the scheme of reinforcement learning. Specifically, we consider the common goal of achieving high performance as social identity, and employ social uniqueness as another motivation in learning. Taking both social identity and uniqueness as constraints, our proposed method results in favorable improvements from both performance and uniqueness perspectives.

    • [NeurIPS 2019 OptRL.W] Policy Evolution with Hindsight Inverse Dynamics
    Hao Sun, Bo Dai, Zhizhong Li, Xiaotong Liu, Rui Xu, Dahua Lin, Bolei Zhou

  • We further extended our work of PCHID into a synchronous setting, namely Policy Evolution with Hindsight Inverse Dynamics (PEHID), where the learning efficiency is further improved. We also show the relation between policy learning for goal-oriented reward sparse tasks and Ornstein-Uhlenbeck (OU) process.
